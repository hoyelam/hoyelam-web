Introducing [CraftCaption](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/craftcaption-ai-hashtags/id6742781620), don't think about social media captions and hashtags anymore. Just provide one or more photos and CraftCaption automatically gives you multiple captions you can use and multiple hashtags to boost your social media post presence.

I built a new application within a week using mainly components from [Xpensemap](https://xpensemap.com/). It was an idea that was 3 years old, but I finally found the time to build a quick version of it.

I love how quickly this new app was built, as it only took a few days while I was taking care of my 10 month old son. Let's see the results of this app while I do some marketing for it as well. Stay tuned.
