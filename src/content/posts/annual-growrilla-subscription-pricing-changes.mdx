Based on the reviews and feedback from users, the biggest complaint was regarding the pricing of Growrilla.

When I’m reflecting on this, the previous pricing of $21.49 USD may have been too much. Some features were still missing and the subscription felt more like a necessity to unlock the whole app. Instead, it should have expanded the experience of Growrilla.

I made a bad call on that last month with the launch, and it stunted my overall growth of the Growrilla. I’m correcting this make by reducing the price massively. The reason for doing this is to ensure the steady and organic growth of Growrilla.

In a month or two I will reflect on the decision, but as of right now, I do think I’m making the right choice.

The new pricing will be $12.49 USD or €13,99 for the annual subscription. The monthly and lifetime subscription pricing is unchanged.
