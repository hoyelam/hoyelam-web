For the past two months, I’ve been working on a project called Mobloc. This was both a web- and app tool to manage localizations for mobile development.

After these two months, I’ve realized that I don’t want to work on this anymore. Feels like it takes too much effort to even work on it daily honestly. Not sure why cause I thought I was one of the people who could use it myself.

But maybe I’m realizing that it’s not even that big of a problem for myself. It’s more of a 'nice to have' solution.

Thus here is the concept I’ve developed, and won’t work on it anymore. Maybe I even open-source it for people to pick it up.

Whatever, I’m not going to work on this anymore.
