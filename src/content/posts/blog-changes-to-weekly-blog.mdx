I’ve given lots of thought to it, and my daily-ish blog will be changed to a weekly one.

Since September, I’ve been writing daily on this blog laying my thoughts down, being an indie developer, and my career choices. My highest back-to-back writing day streak was 141 which is something I’m proud of.

This has been a great habit and experiment that I’ve kept up with for the longest time. But when I’m reflecting upon this, the quality of my daily blog has been going down.

This may have been due to the exhaustion due to other endeavors, but overall, I don’t like the quality of where my blog is going.

Some posts could even have been short Tweets, to sum up, my day, and it felt like I was spamming my blog more than providing interesting content.

So, I’m deciding that this blog will change into a weekly one. Each week, I will blog about my life as an engineer trying to find its way to have a fulfilling career.

This will be a new series as well with the title: “Weekly Update `#1`”

Next to this, I’m going to write more in-depth blog posts more like programming, psychology, and philosophy with no specific set dates or frequency.

With this, I’m aiming to increase the quality of my blog more rather than writing a quick update every day.
