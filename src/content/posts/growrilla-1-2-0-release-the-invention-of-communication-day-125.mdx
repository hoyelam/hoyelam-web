Thought:

Clear communication is the best invention of humankind. This comes in many ways in the form of writing, drawing, and speaking in many different languages.  With communication, experiences and knowledge can be shared through generations and generations.

Stories about the Viking age. Books on Christianity. Drawings about the Egyptian era.

In our age, the most up-to-date version is the ability of broadcasting. You can send messages to people instantly through email, or any social media channel. You can record and edit a video and broadcast your message to the world through YouTube. You can even live-stream your work to the world.

This happens instantly.

Make use of it if you have a clear message that you want to be shared
