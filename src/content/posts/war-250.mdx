Previous global wars started out like this. Providing resources first, then slowly but surely sending human troops to fight in the front lines. Thus participating in a global war.

We don’t know what resources Russia has or even allies that provide them resources as well. We don’t know what Putin is capable of. Don’t trust the media, these ‘journalists’ that analyze things online have never seen the worst. They just write the extremely lay-man expectations. We all are.

Whatever you think, Putin is an extremely dangerous individual and few truly know what he thinks and is capable of.

Once again, this war between Ukraine and Russia could end just between them.

But prepare for the worst. War is not something you and we can take lightly. The consequences of dismissing it could mean death. Or worse, the death of your loved ones.

Mentally and physically prepare for it. Be ready to fight, or at least go AWOL.

This war is going to change our future in a drastic way. There is no turning back.
