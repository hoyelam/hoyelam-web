I’ve pushed out an update immediately but Apple hasn’t approved yet, hopefully by Monday the update is live. This is going to be version 1.0.3.

[De leukste apps van week 51-2020 in App Gemist](https://www.iculture.nl/apps/app-gemist-week-51-2020/).
