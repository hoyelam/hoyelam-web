I broke my daily writing streak for the past three days. Didn't even write in private as well. What happened? Did I stop it entirely?

No.

Last Friday, I completely missed it, cause honestly, I just forgot about it. I was busy with a new app release this September. More on that app another day, but basically forgot about time.

Then on Saturday, I spend 100% of my time with my friends and girlfriend. Literally woke up and then went to meet. We went to yamcha (brunch), chilled in the sun at a park, barbequed in the evening, and played football as well.

One of the rare few times, that I haven't opened my laptop during the whole day. Honestly, I appreciate those days. When I'm financially stable (or free), I want more of those days. Those days are worth every penny.

Then on Sunday, I did work on a few things. Mainly discussing and planning on a new (potential) project that I'm committing myself to starting next year.  However, after coming home, I had burnout symptoms. My whole body hurt due to pain around my neck area. It impacted my body and my mental state. I couldn't move and think at all. So I decided to call it a day and laid in bed.

Slept around 10 hours which could be a year record honestly.

But now I'm back and hopefully rested enough to put myself back in the grind.
