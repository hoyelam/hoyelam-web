I’m putting the daily series on a break again. Since late 2020, I’ve tried to write daily as much as possible. 260 blogs were published during this period with some breaks in between.

But the growth by writing daily has been very stagnant lately. I’m writing just for the sake of writing. My thoughts also haven’t been very clear lately, thus feel like the quality of my writing has taken a hit.

This blog has around 250 unique visitors per month according to the latest analytics. This also has been stagnant in terms of growth, thus am not seeing the upside that much anymore.

Thus I’m taking a break, reflecting and recalibrating what I want to do with my blog.

Maybe I continue next month, maybe I start blogging weekly, maybe I focus entirely on long articles.

I have to reflect and see what’s beneficial for me.

See you then.
