There was this meme I remember. That you were born too early to explore the Earth and too late to explore space, but just in time to browse memes.

I think it resonates with some people, but it never resonated with me. You can always be the stepping stone for anything related to solving a bold and audacious problem.

Today, SpaceX did one of the most amazing things I've ever seen in my life. They sent a rocket into space and brought it back to Earth in one piece. If you don't understand the significance of this breakthrough, you are either arrogant, plain stupid or a piece of shiet.

Still, words can't describe the feeling, so here's the video.

>

I've watched it again and again today. I feel like a kid from the previous generation glued to the television watching the moon landing. But this is here and now.

The impossible is possible today.

It is inspiring and I know all the people at SpaceX are proud, as they must be.

I hope this will encourage further space exploration and inspire the next generation to achieve the impossible. Space exploration and this generation's contribution to it are the stepping stones to a great future.
