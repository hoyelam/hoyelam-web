I love esports.

It’s like watching football, NBA, UFC, or any other sport on TV.

Now the esports season for most videogames has begun. In CS:GO the Blast Premier Global final has begun where the top teams are competing for a million-dollar prize pool.

The LEC, LPL, and LCK season has started in League of Legends as well to win the 2021 Spring Split and qualifying for MSI.

Every day there are interesting matches to watch and storylines to follow. As a fan and somebody who finds it relaxing to watch esports, it is great to have so many options to watch.
