Yes, yes, yes. I’m aiming to have subscriptions for Growrilla. The app to count everything in your life.

With Growrilla premium, you are going to have access to all the features and upcoming ones of Growrilla.

The benefits of a premium subscription are currently as followed:

-   Even more statistics and insights regarding analyzing your counts.
-   Forgot to count an event from last week or earlier? Don’t worry you have the possibility to do correct that.
-   Ability to set reminders for your events that you may want to count (in development, will not be released on launch)
-   Ensure that Growrilla keeps supporting you and build more awesome features.

The pricing is as followed:

-   Monthly Subscription is $3.99
-   Annual Subscription is $24.99
-   Lifetime Subscription is $54.99

The benefits and pricing may change.

More information will be follow on what kind of statistics, chars, and insights you may get from the premium subscription.

As a teaser:

-   Week, month, and year comparisons.
-   Percentage (de-)increases

Stay tuned all!
