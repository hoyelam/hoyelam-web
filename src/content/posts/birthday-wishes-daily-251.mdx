It’s my birthday today and I know usually people wish things for themselves. I don’t do that.

I wish for a strong and peaceful world.

I wish for my family to be healthy and safe.

I wish for unforgettable experiences with my close friends.

I wish that all business of my friends succeed

I wish for many individuals to have many bright days in this world.

I wish you all to suffer for the right things and find happiness.

I wish that others enjoy my presence and value in this world.

Many of these wishes, I’m going to make come true.

Mark my words.
