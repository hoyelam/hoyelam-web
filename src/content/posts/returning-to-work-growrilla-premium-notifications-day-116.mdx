The first workday of 2021 and after a three-week holiday and a new feature coming for Growrilla.

Today, it started out with reading many Slack and email messages. Only 20% of those were relevant for me, but that is how it goes. After an hour or two, the real development started.

The first issue of the day was making all the relevant Xcode projects suitable for M1 Macs. Mostly, because I have an M1 Mac, but future developers won’t have to update the projects. Then some last TODOs regarding some privacy documentation. Overall, a great balanced day.

Nothing too challenging immediately, so that I can slide right back to my productivity levels.

* * *

For Growrilla today, I’ve worked on a new feature that is coming for premium-users. This is the daily notifications for a specific count event.

In the update, you can add daily notifications for a specific event that you are tracking. For example, want to be reminded every-day at 20:30 to write? You will receive a notification for that with Growrilla.

This will be coming next to the total day count view.
