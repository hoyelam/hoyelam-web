I’ve got a three week holiday from my full-time work starting today.

I’m excited.

I haven’t had a long vacation since one and a half years ago.

The only difference is that I can’t travel to another continent.

But whatever at least I got to rest, chill and reflect.

I always suggest to take time off, you can't sustain high output and creativity throughout the year. I have a problem with it as well

Even though, I don’t have any set plans to do during my time off.

I’m mainly focussing on to relax and chill.

Playing some videogames, reading some books, and learning some new things. May looking into doing some small web development or follow a course in Figma design.

These are not set plans, I just do what I like at that moment.

One main objective though is to release Growrilla on 15 December 2020.

Depending on the launch, I may feel incentivized to work on making Growrilla even better.

But the main focus for the rest of December is to rest, chill, plan 2021 and reflect.
