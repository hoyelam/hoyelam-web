I’ve jumped back into working on [Esportsfort](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/esportsfort-matches-results/id6448645001) and just rolled out a fresh update—version 1.9.7!

This release comes with a handful of upgrades and fixes:

-   **New:** Split view mode for iPad—perfect for multitasking fans.
-   Subtle UI tweaks and polish for a smoother experience.
-   Boosted overall performance to keep things running slick.
-   Squashed a pesky bug that sometimes showed the same team roster twice.

I’m not slowing down on Esportsfort anytime soon, so expect more updates ahead. Best part? It’s staying free for the foreseeable future—stick around for what’s next!
