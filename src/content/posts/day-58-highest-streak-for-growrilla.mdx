I’ve announced that I’m working on Growrilla and will be releasing it this year.

Today, I’ve been working on how to calculate the highest streak of the number of counts.

It was a bit complex due to the nature of the database structure that I have. It may also be a bit inefficient for now, but it works.

Tomorrow, I’m going to optimize the algorithm and calculations for performance improvements.

Due to this feature, all upcoming Growrilla users are going to know what their highest streak is of doing something like taking a walk or fasting.

So when somebody asks how many times you’ve done something consecutively, you show them on Growrilla.
