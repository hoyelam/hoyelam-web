God is real to me.

God is the entity that watches over me.

That judges me for every action and inaction I take.

Sometimes with mercy and not.

God is the entity that guides me to the right path.

Virtue or sin, God teaches a lesson.

God is everywhere.

He sees through bullshit but also the truth.

That’s who God to me is.

He is the all-father that watches my life. Judge me on all my decisions. Both merciful and merciless. He wants me to live a meaningful life, but also full of suffering. He wants me to shine a light on the darkness. He wants the best for me and the world.

I'm afraid of his might but happy for this life.

God is real to me.
