You always have them. Days where you felt unproductive, not motivated, or low energy. Sometimes you can't even control it.

Bad news came up, a plan that didn't work out, or an unexpected event happens. You just sometimes have those days.

Nothing you can about them. Nothing wrong about them either.

Unless you can control it.

Just suck it up and try to be better the next day.
