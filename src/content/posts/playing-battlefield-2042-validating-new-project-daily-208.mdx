Missed a few days of writing cause was too busy during the evenings more than before. Also, last weekend, I was playing the new game Battlefield 2042. The game has loads of bugs, but nowadays you just have to accept them. My initial experiences are pretty great.

Next to this, I’m also validating a new project. I’m building a small MVP and trying to make a small ad (TikTok-style) video that explains the pain points it’s trying the solve, the product as a solution, and the benefits from using it. Once this video ad is created, I’m going to validate it with my target audience.

Can’t go into too many details yet in what kind of product it is, cause I’m not the only one working on it. But it is not for tech Twitter, Web3, Swift developers, or self-improvers which are usually my target audience.

Am I excited? Not yet, honestly, the target audience isn’t my community or type of people but is of my partners. The combination of the creator and marketer is a deadly one though, and this will be for me at least a fun and learning experience.
